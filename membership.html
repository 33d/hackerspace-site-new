What do I get from a membership?

Members can attend sessions without paying the fee to attend. And you get to help us run the organization!

Membership special!

Until 31 March 2022, you can get a 12 month membership for $100! If you're already a member, we will extend your membership by 12 months for $100.

How much is membership?

The annual membership is currently $100, it's normally $200. You can pay by Paypal, a bank transfer, or by cash at a session.

Quarterly membership is $70, payable only by a recurring Paypal payment.

You can attend without a membership for $5.
